Our Mission

Eiha Group will educate, inspire, and elevate our community by showcasing the culture and arts of Yemen. Through developing strong communal ties based on our rich culture, history, and our identity as a diaspora, we aim to instill common values to help guide a supportive and uplifting approach to involvement in public life in our community.

Our Vision

Eiha Group seeks to amplify history and culture in the Yemeni diaspora, to orient our self identity and story as we participate in civic life and target the sustainable development of our communities.
